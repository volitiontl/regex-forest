Welcome to Clear Air

Air Quality Monitor

The Air Quality Monitor provides a visual interface for the Air quality of Toronto, GTA & beyond.

What is Clean Air
Air Quality Monitor?

Clean Air provides the visualization of the dataset provided by the goverment of Canada to generate a heat map of localized areas where air quality is effected.

Why care about the air quality in your area?

Air pollution affects all major cities and society, including individual health. It is important to maintain a sustainable environment to make the society cleaner and more fuel efficient.

How can Clean Air help you?

Clean Air can be used to help people understand the effects of air pollution by visualizing the dataset in a particular geographical region by projecting the a visual heatmap. It can be used to monitor and to reduce green house gas emission and air pollution by providing advices to the user as to what they can do to help decrease air pollution in their region.

Helps indicates Air Quality in your region

Shows you a heatmap indicator of the air quality with in your region. The dataset is broken down into intensity on a spectrum from low, being cold colours to warm being the most.

Educate

Helps people understand the health of their city in terms of air quality. The dataset visualizes the city impacted and it's degree with a colour code index based on intensity of the quality of air.

Impact

How as individuals we can impact the air quality of the society that we reside in by taking a bike, buss, and/or walking. Sustainable transport can help reduce global warming, greenhouse gas emission, and air pollution of a city.
